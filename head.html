(Metadata Tags) Provides additional information about the webpage, such as encoding, description, and viewport settings. name="viewport" Common Character Encodings UTF-8 (Recommended): Supports a wide range of characters, including those from many different languages. It is the most widely used and modern encoding. ISO-8859-1: An older encoding for Western European languages. UTF-16: Supports more characters than UTF-8, but less common for web pages. ASCII: Encodes only basic English characters (not suitable for multilingual text).
